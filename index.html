Renee Rudolph is a yoga instructor and teacher working in south central Pennsylvania. Based out of Chambersburg, Renee offers yoga instruction and guidance for all experience levels and believes that yoga is not just a physical practice, but rather a way to maintain balance in life and keep a steady perspective on my life as a part of the whole.

Actively teaching since 2016, Renee offers instruction in Vinyasa flow and aerial yoga. She can be contacted via email, Faceook, or Instagram.
