Renee Rudolph
is a certified yoga instructor and teacher.

Renee offers yoga instruction and guidance for all experience levels guiding students on ways to maintain balance and order and keep a steady perspective on one's life as part of a holistic yogic practice.

Actively teaching since 2016, Renee offers instruction in Vinyasa flow and aerial yoga. She can be contacted via email, Faceook, or Instagram.
